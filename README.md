# Amy

Amy builds consumer web tools for digital rewards, dashboards, engagement and education.

We are building a clearer way for users to understand their activity, rewards, access routes and learning options across digital products and communities.

## What Amy focuses on

- **Dashboards** — clearer views of user activity, positions and available options.
- **Rewards** — points, badges, progress and earned value in one place.
- **Access** — curated routes into partner products, programmes and opportunities.
- **Guidance** — simple educational flows that help users understand before they act.

## Product direction

Amy is designed for products and programmes that need to make rewards, access and learning easier for users to follow.

For users, that means less noise and better context.

For partners, that means clearer routes to better access.

For communities, that means a cleaner way to guide participation, feedback and progress.
